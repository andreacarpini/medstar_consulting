Our Mission

MedStar Consulting was founded in Chicago in July 2024 out of necessity. We witness a healthcare system in the United States that is fundamentally brokena system where exorbitant costs create impossible choices, where insurance companies dictate care, and where life-saving treatments are priced as luxury items.

We see hardworking Americans:

  • Drowning in medical debt for procedures that cost a fraction elsewhere.
  • Denied coverage by insurance providers for essential treatments.
  • Facing endless wait times for specialists while their conditions worsened.
  • Settling for inadequate care or even forgoing treatment altogether due to financial fear.

This is not healthcare. This is a crisis.

Our mission is to shatter these barriers. We are your bridge to a system that prioritizes patients over profits. We connect you with Turkey's world-class healthcare network, where you can access:

  • The sameoften betterquality of care from internationally-trained, top-tier specialists.
  • Cutting-edge technology and state-of-the-art JCI-accredited hospitals.
  • Immediate treatment with little to no waiting time.
  • Transparent, all-inclusive pricing that is 60-80% more affordable than in the U.S., without the surprise bills.

We believe your health should not be a financial burden. Your life-saving surgery shouldn't cost you your life's savings. We've built MedStar to provide a clear, safe, and dignified path to the care you deserve, turning a moment of desperation into one of empowerment.

Your health is priceless. Getting care for it shouldn't be.

Why Turkey?

Turkey has emerged as a global leader in healthcare, boasting state-of-the-art medical facilities, internationally-trained physicians, and cutting-edge technology often at a fraction of the cost of comparable treatments in the United States.

Turkish hospitals consistently achieve JCI accreditation (the gold standard in global healthcare), and many physicians have trained at prestigious institutions across Europe and the United States. The combination of exceptional quality, advanced technology, and significant cost savings makes Turkey an ideal destination for medical care.
